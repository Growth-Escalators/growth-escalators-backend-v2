## Content Brief Template

For each blog post, the writer should follow this structure:

1. **Title** — Include target keyword, use numbers or power words
2. **Meta Description** — 150-160 chars, include keyword + CTA
3. **H1** — Match or closely match the title
4. **Introduction** (200 words) — Hook, problem statement, what they will learn
5. **Body** (1,500-2,500 words) — Use H2/H3 subheadings every 200-300 words
6. **FAQ Section** (5-8 questions) — Use FAQPage schema markup
7. **CTA** — Clear next step (contact form, booking link, download, membership)
8. **Internal Links** — Link to 2-3 related pages on the same site
9. **External Links** — 2-3 authoritative sources (research papers, govt sites)
10. **Images** — 3-5 relevant images with keyword-rich alt text
